Club Deportivo Pozoblanco is a Spanish football team based in Pozoblanco, Córdoba, in the autonomous community of Andalusia. Founded in 1926, it plays in Tercera División – Group 10, holding home matches at Estadio Municipal de Pozoblanco, with a 5,000-seat capacity.[1]
